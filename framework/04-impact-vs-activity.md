# Step 4 — Impact vs Activity

**Pass criteria:** Majority of bullets describe shipped artifacts, not attendance.

## Activity verbs (weak alone)

Attended, Supported, Participated, Helped with, Was involved in, Contributed to (without naming output)

## Impact shape (strong)

`[Verb] [artifact] [collaborator or constraint]`

Examples:
- Built the intake API in Node; paired with design on 8 screens.
- Ran 8 usability sessions; flattened role tree after week two.

## Ratio rule

On page 1, aim for **≥60% impact bullets**. If most bullets are activity, reorder or rewrite.

## Senior caveat

Staff+ resumes may include mentorship and RFC bullets — still name artifacts.
