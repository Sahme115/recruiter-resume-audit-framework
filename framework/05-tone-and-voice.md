# Step 5 — Tone and Voice

**Pass criteria:** Page 1 does not read as LLM-polished corporate copy.

## AI tells

- Symmetric bullet rhythm
- Em-dash on every bullet
- Tier-1 buzzwords (passionate, proven track record, dynamic)
- All bullets 20–25 words (unnatural uniformity)

## Human tells (good)

- One short bullet (~10 words)
- One bullet naming something that went wrong
- Mixed sentence openings
- Specific tool/version names

## Final pass

Read aloud. If you stumble or cringe, rewrite that line.
